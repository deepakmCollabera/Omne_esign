Employment

PLEASE NOTE: Please spend a few extra minutes to review the information below. This will help ensure compliance with client background check requirements. Incorrect or incomplete information can slow down the process and we certainly want your on-boarding to be as easy and painless for you as possible.

PROVIDE COMPLETE INFORMATION:

  • Please include all employers in the past 7 years.
  • Please include all employment including Internships, Voluntary or Military work.

MENTION CORRECT EMPLOYER

Please fill details of your direct employer (on whose payroll you are/were) not the client whose project you worked on. Only mention the employer who paid you directly on W-2 and issued you paychecks.

MENTION CORRECT LOCATION

Project location address has to be the location where you physically worked from.

SELF EMPLOYED DURATIONS

For any period that you were self employed, mention your own company name as the "Employer". In addition, we will need one of the following documents to help complete the Employment check formalities.

  • Letter from your CPA validating that you were self-employed with your company name listed and the duration of employment OR
  • Any company tax filing document for each year that you were self employed indicating your company name. Feel free to blackout any dollar amounts on the returns.
  • Workorder or contract between your own company and your client which shows your company name, duration of self-employment and your signature indicating you were self employed. Feel free to blackout any dollar amounts.

EXCEPTION

Massachusetts applicants do not disclose your prior salry history.

MULTIPLE PROJECTS

If you have worked with two or more employers simultaneously with overlapping time frames, then please enter the longest employment in this Employment section. You may manually email your Collabera Operations Onboarding Executive the other projects that overlapped.

Physical Signature Required

Due to client policy or regulatory reasons, please physically sign
